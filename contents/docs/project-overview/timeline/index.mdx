**Study Duration:** July 1 - November 18, 2025 (20 weeks)

## Timeline Overview

| Phase | Duration | Focus |
|-------|----------|-------|
| **Phase 1: Setup** | July 2025 | Website launch, recruitment |
| **Phase 2: Data Collection** | Aug-Sep 2025 | Interviews and surveys |
| **Phase 3: Analysis** | Oct 2025 | Data analysis, theme development |
| **Phase 4: Completion** | Nov 2025 | Final report, results sharing |

## Detailed Phase Breakdown

### Phase 1: Launch & Recruitment (July 2025)

| Week | Focus | Participant Involvement |
|------|-------|------------------------|
| 1-2 | Website launch, system testing | Sign-ups begin |
| 3-4 | Community outreach, partnerships | Active recruitment |

**Goals:**
• Launch study website and materials
• Begin recruitment through multiple channels
• Test all systems (survey, scheduling, etc.)
• Establish community partnerships

### Phase 2: Data Collection (August - September 2025)

| Month | Interviews | Survey | Milestones |
|-------|------------|--------|------------|
| **August** | 3-5 completed | Distribution expansion | Mid-month assessment |
| **September** | 10-15 total | 200+ responses target | Collection completion |

**Participant Involvement:**
• **Survey**: Open August 1 - September 30
• **Interviews**: Scheduled August 1 - September 30
• **Ongoing**: Regular progress updates shared

### Phase 3: Analysis (October 2025)

**Week 1-2: Initial Analysis**
• Interview transcription completion
• Survey data cleaning and preparation
• Initial theme identification
• Preliminary pattern recognition

**Week 3-4: Deep Analysis**
• Qualitative theme development
• Quantitative analysis and correlation identification
• Cross-referencing findings
• Member checking with select participants

**Participant Involvement:** Optional review of themes for interview participants

### Phase 4: Completion (November 2025)

**Week 1-2: Synthesis**
• Final theme consolidation
• Data visualization creation
• Draft findings preparation
• Academic presentation prep

**Week 3: Final Documentation**
• Research report completion
• Community-friendly summary creation
• Results sharing with participants

**Participant Involvement:** Final findings shared with interested participants

• **October 15**: All transcriptions completed, initial themes identified

- **November 18**: Final academic presentation

## Flexible Elements

### Adaptive Recruitment
If recruitment goals are met early, additional outreach may continue to enhance diversity and sample size.

### Interview Scheduling
Interview timing accommodates participant availability across time zones and schedules, with evening and weekend options available.

### Analysis Timeline
Analysis may begin earlier if data collection proceeds ahead of schedule, allowing more time for thorough theme development.

## Communication Schedule

### Participant Updates
- **Monthly progress emails** to interview participants (August-October)
- **Final findings summary** shared with all participants (December 2025)
- **Appreciation messages** and thank you notes throughout

### Academic Reporting
- **Bi-weekly mentor check-ins** throughout all phases
- **Monthly progress reports** documenting challenges and insights
- **Final presentation** to academic community

## Contingency Planning

### COVID-19 Considerations
All interviews conducted virtually to ensure participant and researcher safety.

### Technology Backup Plans
- Multiple platform options for interviews (Zoom, Google Meet, phone)
- Survey platform alternatives prepared if technical issues arise
- Data backup systems for secure storage

### Timeline Flexibility
Built-in buffer time allows for unexpected delays while maintaining academic calendar alignment.
